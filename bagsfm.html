What is Bags.fm?

A launchpad for creators and builders on Solana

The Platform

Bags.fm is a decentralized launchpad on Solana that allows creators, artists, and builders to launch their tokens and raise funds for their projects — without needing technical expertise or connections.

How It Works

1

Create Your Project

Launch your token on Bags.fm. Set up your project details, tokenomics, and community links.

2

Community Buys In

People discover your project and buy your token. Funds are raised directly to your project.

3

You Claim Fees

Trading fees are generated from your token. You claim 100% of these fees — no middleman, no hidden cuts.

4

Build & Earn

Use the funds to build your project, grow your community, and continue earning from trading fees.

How to Claim Your Fees

One of the best parts of Bags.fm is that you keep 100% of trading fees. Here's how to claim them:

1

Go to Your Project Page

Navigate to your token's page on Bags.fm

2

Connect Your Wallet

Make sure you're connected with the wallet that created the project

3

Click "Claim Fees"

You'll see accumulated trading fees. Click the claim button to withdraw them to your wallet

4

Approve Transaction

Confirm the transaction in your wallet and the fees are yours — instantly

Note: You can claim your fees as often as you want. There's no minimum threshold or waiting period.
