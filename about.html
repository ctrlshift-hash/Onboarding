About Me

The story behind onboarding.dev

My Story

I've been in the Solana ecosystem since early days, watching it grow from a small community of builders to one of the most active chains in crypto. Along the way, I've seen a lot of people try to launch projects and struggle with the technical side of things.

When Bags.fm launched, I saw an opportunity. The platform was powerful but navigating the onboarding process wasn't straightforward for everyone. A few friends asked me for help, and I walked them through it. They launched successfully, raised funds, and started earning.

Word spread. More people reached out. What started as helping a few friends turned into something bigger. Now I've onboarded over 20 builders, helping them collectively raise over $800,000.

Why I Do This

I believe in helping people succeed. The crypto space is full of people trying to take a cut of everything. I wanted to do something different.

When you work with me, you keep 100% of your fees. I don't take a percentage, I don't ask for tokens, I don't have any hidden arrangements. I'm here to help you launch and that's it.

My reward is seeing builders succeed. Building a reputation as someone who actually delivers without strings attached. In a space full of scams and rugs, I want to be the person you can trust.

What I Offer

  • Full onboarding support for Bags.fm
  • Help with project setup and configuration
  • Guidance on launch strategy
  • Ongoing support after launch
  • No fees, no cuts, no hidden costs
